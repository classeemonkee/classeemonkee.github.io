MONKEE THEOREE

Roughly in the year of 420, a group of unruly monkeys roamed Earth, but lived a life of grey routine that revolved on lazing around when they were not eating. All this changed with the unexpected collision between Rodeus and Syno, two planets coming together that would bring about a great change to the DNA of monkeys roaming Earth, through an immense light and substance that travelled space. These affected the monkeys and made them more human (humanoids) blessing them with skills to interact, learn and invent to benefit their generation and the next. What started as simple farming soon evolved to creating patterned clothing and it was not long before they were the apex species of their habitat. Unfortunately, bad omen struck in the form of countless meteors ranging in size from a ping pong to a household refrigerator, which rained upon Earth and wiped out part of it. This resulted in an almost mass extinction. Fortunately, a handful of the monkeys were able to survive the impact and scavenged for sustenance. Due to the finite resources, they turned on each other and split into two gangs called 'Monkey Blood' and 'Monkey Loss'. They lived through a harsh period of infighting and intense rivalry. As a result, the two gangs recruited newcomers. Along with 5555 new monkeys ready to join the battle, get ready to choose your own gang.
